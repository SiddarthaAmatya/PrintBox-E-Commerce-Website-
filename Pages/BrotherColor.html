The Brother HL-T4000DW A3 Color Inkjet Printer stands out for its efficient printing capabilities and high-quality output. Key features include fast printing speeds (up to 35 ppm monochrome and 27 ppm color), a maximum resolution of 1,200 x 4,800 dpi, and a capacity to handle various paper sizes. The model ensures versatile connectivity with Wi-Fi, Ethernet, and USB 2.0.

  • Print Speed: Up to 35 ppm in monochrome and 27 ppm in color for efficient printing
  • High Resolution: Offers up to 1,200 x 4,800 dpi for crisp and detailed print quality.
  • Paper Handling: Supports a variety of paper sizes, catering to versatile printing needs.
  • Connectivity Options: Includes Wi-Fi and Ethernet, enhancing accessibility and ease of use​​​​.
  • Display: Equipped with a 5-line LCD Display for easy navigation and configuration.
  • Duplex Printing: Supports automatic duplex printing.
