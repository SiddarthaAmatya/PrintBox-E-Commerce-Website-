The Canon ImageCLASS LBP226dw is a monochrome laser printer that is known for producing high-quality text, graphics,
and grayscale photos. It's designed to be efficient,
offering a respectable printing speed with low operational costs. The printer supports a variety of features including mobile printing and PCL
compatibility, which are useful for various business needs. Key specifications of the Canon ImageCLASS LBP226dw include:

  • Print Speed: Up to 38 pages per minute (A4 size).
  • First Print Out Time: Approximately 5.5 seconds for A4 size.
  • Print Resolution: Up to 1,200 x 1,200 dpi (equivalent).
  • Connectivity: Offers options like Gigabit Ethernet, Wireless, and Direct Connection.
  • Display: Equipped with a 5-line LCD Display for easy navigation and configuration.
  • Input Capacity: The total input capacity is 250 sheets.
  • Duplex Printing: Supports automatic duplex printing.
