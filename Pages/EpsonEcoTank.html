Specificaiton

The Epson EcoTank ET-4760 is a revolutionary all-in-one printer that stands out for its super-high-capacity, refillable ink tanks, which replace traditional cartridges. This design significantly reduces ink costs over time and is environmentally friendly. It's a great choice for small businesses or home offices with high print volumes. The printer offers robust functionality including printing, scanning, copying, and faxing. It also features an automatic document feeder for efficiency and supports wireless printing, making it easy to print from smartphones and tablets.

  • Print Technology: PrecisionCore Heat-Free Technology (Inkjet)
  • Connectivity Options: Wi-Fi Direct, Ethernet, USB 2.0, and remote printing with Epson Connect
  • Functions: Print, Scan, Copy, Fax
  • Print Resolution: Up to 4800 x 1200 optimized dpi
  • Print Speed: 15 ppm (black), 8 ppm (color)
  • Paper Handling: 250-sheet input tray, 30-sheet ADF, supports legal, letter, A4, B5, A6, half letter, envelopes
  • Display: 2.4" color touchscreen
  • Ink System: EcoTank ink bottles
  • Dimensions: 14.8" x 13.7" x 9.1
