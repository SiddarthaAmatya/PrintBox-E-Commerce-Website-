The HP DeskJet 2320 All-in-One Printer is a straightforward and efficient solution for home printing needs. It offers a seamless and easy setup, particularly favored by users who prefer uncomplicated technology. Ideal for moderate home use, this printer combines the functions of printing, scanning, and copying in a compact design, suitable for small spaces. Its user-friendly features and reliable performance make it a practical choice for everyday printing tasks.

  • Print Technology: Thermal inkjet
  • Connectivity Options: USB port (USB Type-A), no Wi-Fi or Ethernet LAN
  • Functions: Print, Copy, Scan, Scan to PDF
  • Print Resolution: Up to 4800 x 1200 DPI (color), 1200 x 1200 DPI (black)
  • Print Speed: Up to 7.5 ppm (black, normal quality, A4/US Letter), 5.5 ppm (color, normal quality, A4/US Letter)
  • Paper Handling: 60-sheet input capacity, 25-sheet output capacity
  • Display: No built-in display, control type is buttons
