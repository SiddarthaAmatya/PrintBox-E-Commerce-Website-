The Lexmark MC3224dwe is a color laser printer that stands out for its compact design and high-quality color prints. It's also energy efficient and has a relatively fast print speed for its category.

  • Print Speed: Up to 24 ppm (black and color)

  • Dimensions: 16.2" x 15.5" x 13.6"
