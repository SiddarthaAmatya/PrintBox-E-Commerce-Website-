The Xerox B215 is a compact and affordable monochrome laser printer. It's an excellent choice for small teams and home offices, offering reliable and consistent performance. This printer excels in producing crisp and clear text documents. It includes features like a touchscreen interface, wireless printing, and an automatic document feeder. Its compact size makes it a good fit for environments where space is at a premium.

  • Functions: Print, Scan, Copy, Fax
  • Print Resolution: Up to 1200 x 1200 dpi
  • Print Speed: Up to 31 ppm (black)

  • Display: 3.5" touchscreen
