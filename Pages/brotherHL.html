Brother HL-L2395DW

Specificaiton

The Brother HL-L2395DW is a reliable monochrome laser printer perfect for busy homes or small offices. It focuses on efficient performance with fast printing speeds and a straightforward, no-nonsense design. This model includes a flatbed scan glass for convenient copying and scanning. It also supports wireless printing and is known for its cost-effectiveness, especially in terms of toner use. The lack of color printing is offset by its robust build and consistent performance.

  • Print Technology: Electrophotographic laser (monochrome)
  • Connectivity Options: Wi-Fi, NFC, Hi-Speed USB 2.0
  • Functions: Print, Scan, Copy
  • Print Resolution: Up to 2400 x 600 dpi
  • Print Speed: Up to 36 ppm
  • Paper Handling: 250-sheet capacity lower paper tray, manual feed slot, 50-sheet ADF
  • Display: 2.7" color touchscreen
  • Dimensions: 16.1" x 15.7" x 10.7"
