Specificaiton

The Canon PIXMA TR8620 is an all-in-one printer designed with home office users in mind. It offers exceptional ease of use and versatility. This printer excels in printing high-quality photos and documents, making it a good choice for both work-related tasks and creative projects. The TR8620 comes with an intuitive touchscreen interface, an automatic document feeder for quick scanning, and a built-in fax. Its connectivity options include Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Ethernet, ensuring seamless integration with various devices and networks.

  • Print Technology: ChromaLife100 Inkjet
  • Connectivity Options: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.0 LE (Low Energy), Ethernet, USB, AirPrint, Mopria Print Service
  • Functions: Print, Scan, Copy, Fax
  • Print Resolution: Up to 4800 x 1200 dpi
  • Print Speed: Up to 15 ipm (black), 10 ipm (color)
  • Paper Handling: Front paper cassette: 100 sheets of plain paper, Rear paper tray: 100 sheets of plain paper or 20 sheets of 4"x6" photo paper
  • Display: 4.3" LCD touchscreen
  • Dimensions: 17.3" x 13.8" x 7.5"
